About me

Words for pet lovers, by a pet lover.

I’m a lifelong animal enthusiast and full time wordsmith. I cut my teeth volunteering at rescue shelters, where I learned how to soothe anxious pups, bottle-feed orphan kittens, and translate a thousand wags and whisker twitches into plain English.

Over the past six years I’ve written: training columns for Pawsitive Life, product copy for eco-friendly toy brands, and long-form features on topics ranging from canine arthritis to the secret social lives of rabbits. My work blends journalistic research with first-hand experience, so readers feel both informed and understood. When I’m not at the keyboard, you’ll find me clicker-training my senior Lab, ghost-writing adoption profiles for the local shelter, or editing blog posts with a calico curled across my lap.
